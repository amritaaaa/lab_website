Hello, Myself

Dr. Piyush Lal

And I'm a Research Faculty|

Dr. Piyush Behari Lal, has recently joined as a full-time research faculty. He has 13 years of international research and teaching experience in the field of Microbiology. He has obtained his B. Sc. (Hons) degree in Biological Techniques from L. N. Mithila University as a University topper, M. Sc. degree in Biomedical Sciences from Bundelkhand University, Jhansi, U.P. Thereafter, he moved to USA for an integrated MS-Ph.D. degree at the University of Texas at Dallas in the specialty of molecular and cell biology and postdoctoral training at the University of Wisconsin – Madison, WI, USA, where he continued to work as an Assistant Scientist for the Great Lakes Bioenergy Research Center. His PhD dissertation involved finding redundancy and promiscuity of aminotransferases in Escherichia coli and postdoctoral work involved discovering barriers of genetics in a nonmodel microbe Zymomonas mobilis. .
